🔒 Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026. We take your privacy seriously.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you register, we collect your name, email address, and password (hashed, never stored in plain text).

Trading Data

We store your trade history, watchlist, settings, and performance data to provide the service. This data is stored in our secure database and used only to operate the Platform.

Broker Credentials

If you connect a broker, your API keys are stored encrypted in our database. We never store your broker account passwords. API keys are used solely to execute trades on your behalf.

Usage Data

We collect standard server logs including IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps. This is used for security monitoring and platform improvement.

Communications

If you contact us, we retain your messages to respond and improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and provide the Libera AI Platform
  • To execute trades through your connected broker account
  • To send service-related emails (trade alerts, account notifications)
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security incidents
  • To improve and develop new features
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties. We do NOT use your trading data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Sharing

We share your information only in these circumstances:

  • Broker Integrations: Your API keys are shared with your chosen broker to execute trades
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (AWS), email delivery, and monitoring services under strict data processing agreements
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or to protect rights and safety
  • Social Features: Trade broadcasts you choose to share publicly are visible to other platform users

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • AES encryption for sensitive data at rest (API keys, credentials)
  • bcrypt password hashing — we cannot recover your password
  • JWT authentication with short expiry windows
  • Regular security audits and monitoring

Despite these measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your trade history and account data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@liberaai.com

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Trade history is retained indefinitely for performance analytics unless you request deletion. Server logs are retained for 90 days.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication (JWT tokens) and session management. See our Cookie Policy for details.

8. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe we have collected data from a minor, contact us immediately.

9. Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@liberaai.com
